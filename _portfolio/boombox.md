## Explanation
Bömbox lets you get in touch with your choice of independent artists' who can create customized playlists for you. With an in-app messenger, Bömbox allows artists to get to know their audience on a personal level and deliver quality to their audience’s expectations. In addition, the two-way rating system creates trusts and ensures respect from both ends of the community. We aim to create seamless communication and collaboration, making playlist customization and music sharing a positive experience.
